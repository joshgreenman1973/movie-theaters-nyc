When the lights went out

Every movie house New York could lose, lit on the map. Press play and watch a century of theaters open, glow and go dark — each fading to an ember where it once stood.

How this was made — read before trusting a number

  • Theaters, locations, seat counts and statuses come from Cinema Treasures, a crowdsourced, user-edited database. Some entries are incomplete or wrong.
  • Opening and closing years were extracted by AI from each theater's written description, with a confidence rating. Many are approximate; some are unknown and those theaters are left off the timeline (counted under “undated”).
  • Seat counts are user-entered and unverified, though the famous houses check out.
  • This is not a complete census of every theater that ever existed in the city — only what the source records.
  • See the methodology for full detail.
